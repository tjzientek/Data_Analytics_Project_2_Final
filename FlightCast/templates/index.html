Welcome to Flightcast


Flightcast is your optimal place to obtain data on any airline or flight. It is a one stop location comparison site for you to determine which airline would have you to your final destination in the optimal time and with minimal delays.

We have pulled API data from AllAirports JSON into a sqlite database to populate a table of all airport origins and destinations within the United States.
